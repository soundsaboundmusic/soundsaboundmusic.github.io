About

My name is Matt Kresge and I’ve been playing, writing and performing music for over 20 years. I started Willafunk Productions to pursue a career doing what I enjoy most – making music. But starting my own production company isn’t exactly something that happens overnight. Willafunk Productions is the culmination of my musical journey that began 20 years ago when I discovered an old Yamaha acoustic guitar tucked away in a living room closet.  Since that fateful day, I’ve had the pleasure of fulfilling many of my musical dreams and visions: performing in front of a sold-out crowd at Philadelphia’s coveted Theater of the Living Arts, opening for musical heroes like Funk/Fusion pioneer Bernie Worrell, hearing my music broadcasted nationally over television airwaves, even winning ‘Best Guitarist in Philly’ at the 2005 Emergenza Festival.   It has been an exciting musical ride….one that I hope is far from over!   Let Willafunk Productions play a small part in helping you realize your dreams and visions!
