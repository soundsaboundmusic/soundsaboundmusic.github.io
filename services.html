Lessons

Based in Plymouth Meeting, PA, Sounds Abound Music offers private in-home guitar and drum lessons throughout the Northwest Philadelphia suburb region. Think of the convenience and time saved by having an instructor come to your home! Please refer to the area map on the 'CONTACT' page for our general area of service or to inquire about lessons in your area.


* Students of all ages accepted.

RATE:
* 1 payment cycle (4 half hour lessons) = $125
* 10% family discount rate offered for multiple lessons in one household
