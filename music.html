  • Bio

    I have been performing live for over 15 years. In addition to winning ‘Best Guitarist’ at the 2005 Emergenza Festival in Philly, I’ve had the pleasure of playing all over the greater Philly area. My acoustic repertoire covers all areas of popular music from the 50s through today: classic rock, modern rock, classic country, bluegrass and everything in between. What sets me apart from other performers is the full band sound I’m able to achieve thanks to a customized loop pedal I use as part of my live gear. It’s not uncommon for me to kick a percussive beat, bass groove, and rhythm guitar simultaneously throughout various songs in my repertoire. Capturing the attention and curiosities of a crowd is typical during my performances. Please be sure to to check out my “Pink Panther Theme” demo to hear how the loop is built to create a full band sound! I offer nothing but a professional approach to the gigs that I book. The numerous weddings, private affairs and sizable list of establishments I’ve performed for is a testiment to this: Duffers Pub (Chadds Ford) PJ Whelihans (Blue Bell) Magerks (Ft. Washington) Spring House Tavern (Spring House/Ambler) Totaros (Conshohocken) Union Jacks (Manayunk) The Drake (Jenkintown) Ryans Pub (West Chester) Chadds Ford Winery (Chadds Ford) Kurtz Creek Winery (Avondale) Scoogies (Flourtown)
